About

Clees' Weapon’s Refrain (Ultimate) and Unending Coil of Bahamut (Ultimate) guides are invaluable resources to learn some of Final Fantasy 14’s most difficult fights. Originally hosted on Clees' own site at https://clees.me, they are reproduced here by Veraticus with the author’s permission, given that no advertising is added to the content.

These guides can be altered on GitHub! Please contribute to keep them as relevant as possible.
